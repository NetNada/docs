This is your guide to getting started with NetNada. If you’re new to carbon accounting software for your business, this is the guide you should start with

## What is NetNada?

NetNada is a sustainability and carbon management platform, that helps you and your business decarbonise.

With NetNada, you can track emissions and then use that information to implement strategies, achieve certification, engage your suppliers or just generally improve your companies sustainability stance.

## 1. Selecting a reporting year
Emissions measurement are usually measurend and  reported for a 12 month period. This period can be for a calendar year or a financial year.

### Calendar year reporting 

Calendar year is defined from January 1st to December 31st.

### Financial year reporting

Financial year is defined from July 1st to June 30th.

## 2. Setting up your organisation

The first step is to set up what part of your organisation is going to be measured.

This can be all, or only parts of it.

This is also know as defining your 'organisational boundaries' as per the GHG protocol.

### Organisation Nodes

To facilitate with setup, NetNada offers the following nodes.

- Business
- Locations

<Note>You can have 1 business but you can add as many locations as you would like. This could be for example **offices, warehouses, production plants, and retail stores**.</Note>

### Business Name
Name associated with your business identification number such as ABN.

### Business description
Short description that identifies the products or services offered by your business. Can include things such as business motto and founded date.
